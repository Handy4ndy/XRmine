Key Features

With our innovative approach to cryptocurrency mining, we offer rewards in digital currencies and assets to users in exchange for mining data from the best projects built on the ledger.

Our ambition is to bridge the gap between seasoned veterans and those new to the ecosystem by providing the same reward structure to all. This is done while adopting and promoting key features of the XRPL Blockchain.

These features underscore XRmine's dedication to creating a system that efficiently distributes tokens and cultivates meaningful, long-term relationships between our users and the diverse projects within the XRPL.

Self Custody:

XRmine prioritizes user control utilizing the Xaman wallet app (formally known as Xumm), this ensures a secure environment where you are in control of your assets at all times.

Users can sign in and submit payloads(transactions) with the user friendly interface that is Xaman, the non-custodial wallet app Built by XRPLabs!

The XRP Ledger:

Building on the XRP Ledger allows us to take advantage of many benefits it has over other Blockchains, able to process 1500 transactions every 3.3 seconds through a decentralised consensus network it outperforms it key competitors significantly.

Another key feature of XRPL is the transaction fees, which make it highly cost-effective to move funds across the network, currently costing fractions of a cent.

Sustainability:

XRmine revolutionizes traditional mining by eliminating the need for expensive hardware, thereby reducing energy consumption.

Our platform, accessible via any browser, represents a sustainable mining solution in line with XRPL's commitment to green blockchain technology.

Consensus Mechanism:

We are committed to community-driven decisions. We're implementing a consensus voting mechanism, allowing users to actively vote for the projects they want to see listed and the updates they want us to bring.

Your engagement through our social channels and direct messages help shape our platform, fostering a community-driven ecosystem.
